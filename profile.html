Regional Area Health (Ralth)

“Equitable Health, Primary Welfare”

Ralth is an integrated platform committed to becoming a milestone main role in supporting the Ministry's Healthy Archipelago Program Health, with the vision of realizing inclusive access to health, integrative, and at a level for every Indonesian citizen. Our startup reflects a commitment to improving health and welfare in certain areas or regions may be remote or have limited access to health services. "Ralth" is a symbol of the effort to create positive change and provide quality health services in communities community in need. In this context, "Ralth" creates an overview of a company with a healthcare focus that cares about local health needs, addressing accessibility challenges, and provide comprehensive solutions to improve welfare public.

Vision

"Provider of inclusive, integrative and shared health access all levels of Indonesian society

Mission

Supporting the Nusantara Sehat Program We are committed to supporting the Nusantara Sehat Program by providing better access to health services in remote areas by ordering specialist doctors

Improving Health Access Our platform is a health bridge that connects patients in remote areas with specialist doctors
